### **ğŸ•°ï¸ What is Time Series Forecasting?**  
Time series forecasting involves analyzing historical data to predict future outcomes using **statistical**, **mathematical**, and **machine learning** methods. In this competition, it served as a critical tool for improving traffic management.  

While often used interchangeably, **forecasting** and **prediction** differ slightly:  
- **Forecasting**: Focuses on predicting future outcomes with specific timeframes.  
- **Prediction**: Offers general future estimates without time constraints.
