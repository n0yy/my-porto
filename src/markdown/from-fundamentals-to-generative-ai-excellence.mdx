### 🌐 Overview of the Bangkit Academy Program

Bangkit Academy is an **intensive, interdisciplinary program** designed to equip students with in-demand technology skills. In 2024, the program offered three learning paths:

1. 💻 **Machine Learning**
2. 📱 **Mobile Development**
3. ☁️ **Cloud Computing**

Participants engaged in:
- 🧑‍💻 Asynchronous online learning via platforms like **Dicoding Academy** and **Coursera**
- 🤝 Interactive discussions with industry experts
- 🧑‍🏫 **Mentorship support** for personal and professional growth
